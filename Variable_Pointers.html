Aryithmetic on variable pointers

Generally pointers can be viewed as an one dimensional stream of bytes. You can only step forwards and backwards depending on the step size which is determined by the datatype.

Datatype Size
char 1 byte
int 2 - 4 byte
long 8 or 4 byte (32Bit OS)
float 4 byte
double 8 byte
long double 10 byte
