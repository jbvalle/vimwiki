Unix Philosophy

  • Unix was mainly designed for developers and programmers
  • Unix is made of small simple programs that allow chaining by piping
  • A complex task can be performed by executing a sequence of simple programs
  • Each program should be able to pass output as the input of another program
  • Unix is textorientated and configurations are done by textfiles.
  • Simplicity over complexity and perfection
  • Programs should be portable over different unix systems
