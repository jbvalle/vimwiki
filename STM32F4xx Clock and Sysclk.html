Clock and Sysclk

Clock Sources

  • On Chip RC Clock
  • External Crystal oscillator

SYSCLK

  • The systemclock runs the cpu and peripherals such as the ADC, Timer, UART, etc.
  • The Systemclock's source is either the internal clock source or the external clock source or the Phase Lock Loop PLL. there are:
    • HSI (High Speed Internal) RC Oscillator clock
    • HSE (High Speed External) oscillator clock using crystal value of 4 to 26MHz
    • One of the outputs of the PLL Circuit
